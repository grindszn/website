Vex Robotics

One of my proudest projects was working with the Women In Engineering Vex robotics team. The vex challange is a middle school through college robotics comptition that brings computer science and computational thinking to life. I worked with the computer science team to help design and program a robot using VEX parts and C++ programming language. I learned how to navigate github and work with a team of powerful women to build our design into fruition. In 2020 for the first time in our team's history our VEX-U robotics team won the Excellence award in Houston, and qualified for the world Championships in Louisville Kentucky.However, it was cancelled due to covid.This project provided me hands on experience with improving my technical skills.

Studytimekeeper

A personal project of my own that I really enjoyed was working on building my own app. This project I begun in high school as a way of showing my interest in Computer Science. Now, it is a fully published app on the android app store that allows students to track the amount of time they have spent working on each subject for school. This app helped me keep track of the amount of time I was working on my SAT preperation and other course material.I learned how to navigate and use android studio and develop my skills with programming through this project. I was able to build my java project through the utilization of Android studio and gain more in depth understanding of building in parts and testing code rather than attempting to run it all at once.
